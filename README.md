UBopoly is an AI-powered, campus-themed adaptation of the classic Monopoly game set at the University at Buffalo. 
Players navigate a live interactive map, purchasing and trading real UB landmarks such as Capen Hall, Knox Hall, and the Student Union. 
A AI-driven banker manages turns, transactions, and in-game dialogue, bringing humor and intelligence to the gameplay. 
Built with React, TailwindCSS, Flask, and Mapbox, UBopoly transforms the UB campus into an engaging digital board game that blends strategy, technology, and school spirit.
